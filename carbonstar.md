# 6" RC Apertura Carbonstar notes

## Prime focus with just 533MC:
Note no filter wheel here,.

All 3 big extnders, then focuser.  
Take off silver ring on back.  
Screw on M56-48T adapter.  
Then all three 533MC rings.  T42-t42 first, then 
Focus point here is about 12mm.

## Prime focus with 2x powermate.
Okay, that's cool...it *is* parafocal, if you do it right!
All 3 big extenders, then focuser.
No silver ring, do the m56-48T adapter on the scope.
Then screw in the powermate to that adapter.
DONT take off the televue barrel...but you need to take off one of the rings on the camera to get it to go in all the way.  (I took off the M42-M48 adapter)
Focus point about 12mm.

Daytime exposure needed:  about 40ms at 100 gain.

## prime focus with 4x powermate
Soooo....lets just try replacing the 2x with the 4x!!!
Okay, not quite parafocal here, but still in range!!!
